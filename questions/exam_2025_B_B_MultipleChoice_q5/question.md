## Question
מה הקשר העקרוני בין המחלקה `Human` (אדם) למחלקה `Heart` (לב)

### Options
- Composition
- Association
- Dependency
- Aggregation

## Answer
Composition represents a strong 'has-a' relationship where the part (Heart) cannot exist independently of the whole (Human). If the Human is destroyed, the Heart is also destroyed.
